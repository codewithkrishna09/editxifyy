Refund Policy

Effective: Jan 2026 Financial Protection

1. General Principle

Due to the nature of digital creative work, time and labor are non-returnable. Once our editors have commenced work on your timeline, we cannot offer a full refund for the hours already spent.

Our goal is your satisfaction, which is why we provide revision rounds to fix issues rather than cancelling projects.

2. Cancellation Window & Kill Fee

If you wish to cancel a project, the refund amount depends on the project stage:

  • 0-2 Hours after order: 100% Refund (If work hasn't started).
  • Work Started (Draft Phase): 50% Refund (Project Kill Fee applies).
  • First Draft Delivered: No Refund. Revisions will be provided.

3. Non-Refundable Scenarios

Refunds are NOT granted for subjective reasons or changes in direction:

  • "I decided I don't want a video anymore."
  • "I don't like the style" (if it matches the portfolio/brief provided).
  • Delays caused by the client not providing footage/feedback on time.

4. Eligibility for Full Refund

We take responsibility for our mistakes. You are eligible for a 100% refund if:

  • We fail to deliver the project within the agreed timeframe (without prior notice).
  • We are unable to complete the project due to technical issues on our end.
  • The final delivery is technically corrupted or unusable.

5. Dispute Resolution

If you believe you are entitled to a refund outside these terms, please contact number 9696554268. We review cases manually and aim for a fair resolution within 48 hours.
